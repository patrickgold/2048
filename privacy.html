What personal data is collected?

No personal data is collected on this website.

Are cookies stored on my device? If yes, how are they used?

This website stores cookies to give you a continuous experience over multiple game sessions. No personally-identifiable information is included. You can change your cookie choice at any time by clicking on the cookie icon in the top right corner of the website.

The following list shows all cookies which are always being stored on your device and explains their use: 
  • cookiesAccepted
    To tell wether you have previously accepted cookies. This cookie will always be set to automatically hide the cookie banner based on your previous choice.
The following list shows all cookies which will only be stored if you accept the cookie statement and explains their use: 
  • gameSession
    Saves the current game session as a JSON-formatted string.
  • highscore
    Saves the current highscore as a number.
  • theme
    Saves the current theme name as a string.
